Banana Crepes

Ingredients (*Makes 10 crepes*):

  • 1/4 cup ripe pureed banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 3/8 cup flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
  • pinch of cloves
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Prep Process

  1. In a processor, blend the banana until there are no lumps
  2. Add the warm, melted butter and blend
  3. Mix in the rest of the ingredients and blend until smooth
  4. Transfer batter into another bowl and cover top with plastic inside the fridge, and let it sit for a minimum of one hour (Max 4)
  5. Once chilled, mix the batter again by hand for a few seconds

Cooking Time!

  1. Heat a medium pan over a medium heat. Add a thin bit of butter evenly to the pan
  2. Pour a little of the batter on the pan and immediately rotate the pan so that it is distributed thinly and evenly into a circle
  3. Let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes before flipping the crepe. Watch for tiny bubbles forming as an indicator. The cooked side should be a warm brown
  4. Cook the other side for 30 seconds or as long as necessary
  5. Slide the crepe to a clean plate and repeat the above with the rest of the batter
  6. Enjoy crepes with melted chocolate and sliced fruits or plain!
